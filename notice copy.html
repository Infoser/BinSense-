Government Fines for Littering

  • ₹500 fine for throwing garbage on roads or public places.
  • ₹1,000 fine for dumping waste in water bodies.
  • ₹2,000 fine for spitting or urinating in public spaces.
  • ₹5,000 fine for illegal waste burning.
  • Business establishments found littering may face penalties up to ₹10,000.
  • Repeated offenders may face higher fines or legal action.
Please keep our environment clean. Dispose of waste responsibly.
